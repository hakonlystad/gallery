The Meantime Stories

  • e-book cover
  • For Svingen & Pedersen

The Meantime Stories, by writer duo Vegard Svingen & Øyvind Pedersen, is a funny, and rather wacky short read series that draws inspiration from Terry Pratchett, Monty Python, and Douglas Adams.

The series was published in english and norwegian, so the title was needed in two versions.

This is the norwegian cover.
