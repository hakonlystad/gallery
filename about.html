I am a versatile illustrator, concept artist and designer with more than 20 years of experience working across different styles, genres and techniques. I have worked on a range of different types of assignments, including children’s books, book covers, editorial illustrations, ads, natural history, different sorts of educational illustration and concept art. My clients include Gyldendal, Cappelen Damm, Vigmostad & Bjørke, Kunnskapsforlaget, a-magasinet, Samlaget, EnterTeller Media, Læringssentralen, Bladkompaniet, Norsk Folkeminnelag, Odfjell Drilling, Icelandic Institute of Lexicography, Westermann Bildungsmedien Verlag.

I enjoy developing different visual expressions for different assignments. As the the gallery probably makes evident, I also enjoy working with subjects of fantasy and science fiction.

Currently I am studying Media and Interaction Design at the University of Bergen. I coded this gallery page with html, css and javascript (it's still work in progress).
